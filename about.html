About CarbonTracker

CarbonTracker is a comprehensive web application designed to help you understand and reduce your carbon footprint. Our mission is to empower individuals and communities to make informed decisions and contribute to a more sustainable future.

The application features user authentication, allowing you to seamlessly log in, track your daily activities, and monitor your carbon emissions. With intuitive CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) functionalities, you can easily manage your activity logs and visualize your progress using dynamic Chart.js charts.

Our team of dedicated developers, Vinay Kumar Moluguri, Kashyap Kacharlakota, Rishika Nidiganti, and Bhavana Tadepalli, have worked tirelessly to create a user-friendly and feature-rich platform. We've incorporated additional features, such as a hero section, image links, an FAQ accordion, and a sleek Bootstrap footer, to enhance the overall user experience.

We're committed to continually improving and expanding the capabilities of CarbonTracker, so that it remains a valuable tool for individuals and communities on their journey to a greener future.

Features
1.User Authentication: User registration and login functionality.
2.CRUD Operations: Create, read, update, and delete operations for calculating and managing carbon footprint reduced.
3.Carbon Footprint Tracking: Log and track daily food intake with details such as name, quantity, meal, and date.
4.Dynamic Chart.js Visualizations: Real-time visualizations of carbon footprint reduced over time for a comprehensive view of carbon footprint reduction and contribution to the impact on climate change. Chartjs
